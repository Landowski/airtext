01What is Airtext?
+
Airtext is a browser extension that allows you to replace typed shortcuts with pre-defined texts, increasing your productivity.
02How does it work?
+
You define shortcuts like "**email**" and assign long texts like "myemail@example.com". When you type the shortcut, the long text is automatically inserted.
03Why use a text expander?
+
Text expanders are a powerful productivity tool for increasing business and personal productivity. By automating repetitive typing tasks, they save time and effort, allowing you to focus on higher-level activities.

Companies can create standardized snippets or keyboard shortcuts for frequently used phrases, canned responses, automate data entry, email templates for meeting requests, and even product descriptions, ensuring brand consistency across teams . This not only saves time, but also ensures accuracy and professionalism in customer communication.

Personal Benefits: Increases operational efficiency, personal productivity and personalizes communication much faster than you can type it all. Individuals can leverage text expanders for frequently used email greetings, addresses, or even complex signatures. Additionally, text expanders can be used to store and insert custom snippets like jokes, inspirational quotes, or disclaimers, adding a touch of personality to your communication.
04Where do i install Airtext?
+
Just click on the buttons on the website and you will be redirected to the Chrome Web Store, the Google Chrome extension store.

There, just click the "Add" button and the extension will be ready to use.

In the browser, click on the puzzle icon at the top right and click on the pin icon next to the Airtext text, and then the extension will be pinned to the browser's bookmark bar. Just click on the hurricane icon to open the app, register and start inserting your shortcuts and categories.
